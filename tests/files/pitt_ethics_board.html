Ethics Hearing Board Meetings

The City of Pittsburgh Ethics Hearing Board meets at 4:00 p.m. the second Thursday of most months, with supplemental meetings regularly scheduled to occur on the fourth Thursday of the month, as needed. The scheduled meeting dates for this year are listed below. Meetings occur in Room 646 of the City-County Building, which is accessible by the elevators on the Grant-Street-side of the building. Please note meeting minutes are posted here after they are approved by the Ethics Hearing Board at its next meeting.
 
Questions and requests for accommodation should be directed to Executive Manager Leanne Davis at 412-255-2122 or by email.
